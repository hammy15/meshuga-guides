## PAGE 20: When to Use Both Together

**28 / Two-Pass Workflow**

**Pass 1 (ChatGPT):** Fast draft, broad coverage, multiple options  
**Pass 2 (Claude):** Refine, add depth, improve tone, remove clichés

**This is the secret.** Don't pick one. Use both in sequence.

---

**29 / Parallel Comparison**

Ask the SAME question to both. Compare answers.

**When to do this:**
- Important decisions
- Unfamiliar topics
- High-stakes content
- Checking for hallucinations

**You'll notice:** ChatGPT gives you confidence fast. Claude makes you think twice (usually for the better).
